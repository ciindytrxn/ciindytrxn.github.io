Room Occupancy Detection Using IoT Sensor Data and Machine Learning

Building energy consumption accounts for approximately 40% of total energy usage in developed countries, with Heating, Ventilation, and Air Conditioning (HVAC) systems and lighting representing the largest contributors to this consumption. A significant portion of this energy, estimated at 20-30%, is wasted due to inefficient operation of these systems in unoccupied spaces. Traditional occupancy detection methods, primarily relying on passive infrared (PIR) motion sensors, suffer from fundamental limitations including high false negative rates for stationary occupants, sensitivity to environmental factors, and inability to provide occupancy density information.

Left-Right Media Portrayal of the 2023 Government Shutdown

Content analysis of left-right media coverage examining the agenda-setting and framing in news coverage surrounding the 2023 government shutdown. Through comprehensive analysis of 252 news articles from Left, Right, and Centrist media outlets, this study compares how each venue frames the event using six generic framing categories: conflict, human-interest, economic impact, morality, responsibility, and nationalization.
